Welcome to the Intel Timeline

Explore the groundbreaking innovations and milestones that have defined Intel's legacy in the world of technology.

Learn More

1971: Intel 4004

The world’s first commercially available microprocessor.

2003: First Sustainability Report

Intel publishes its first corporate responsibility report, highlighting environmental and social progress.

2008: Green Power Leadership

Intel becomes the largest voluntary purchaser of green power in the U.S.

2012: Water Conservation

Intel achieves significant water savings, recycling billions of gallons annually.

2018: 100% Green Power in U.S.

All U.S. Intel operations powered by 100% renewable energy.

2020: RISE Strategy

Intel launches RISE, a 2030 strategy for responsible, inclusive, and sustainable growth.

2022: Net Positive Water

Intel restores more water than it consumes in U.S. operations, achieving net positive water use.

2023: Zero Waste to Landfill

Multiple Intel sites achieve zero waste to landfill certification.

About Us

Intel is a global leader in semiconductor innovation, powering the world’s digital infrastructure. Since its founding in 1968, Intel has been at the forefront of technological advancement, delivering breakthrough products and driving progress in computing, networking, data storage, and communications. Committed to sustainability and responsible business practices, Intel continues to shape the future with a focus on innovation, inclusion, and environmental stewardship.
